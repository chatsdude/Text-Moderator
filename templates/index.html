Why content moderation?

With content moderation, your team can gain actionable insights about the behavior and opinions of your users and customers.



A scalable content moderation process lets you publish high volumes of user-generated content while protecting your reputation and
your customers.

Okay,but why AI?

According to the World Economic Forum, by 2025,
an estimated 463 exabytes of data will be created each day.
That's the equivalent of 2.1 million DVDs!



The sheer quantity of user-generated content makes
it difficult for humans to keep pace.


This is why using AI as an aid for human agents, rather than
a replacement, is ideal.
